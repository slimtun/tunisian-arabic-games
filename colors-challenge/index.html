🇹🇳 Learn Tunisian Derja

Master Arabic Noun-Color Gender Agreement!

In Tunisian Arabic, colors change depending on whether the object is masculine (m) or feminine (f). Learn the rules, study the illustrations, and test your skills!

WHITE
m: abyath
f: bitha
BLACK
m: ak7al
f: ka7la
RED
m: a7mar
f: 7amra
BLUE
m: azraq
f: zarqa
GREEN
m: akhthar
f: khathra
YELLOW
m: asfar
f: safra
